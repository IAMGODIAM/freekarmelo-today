Sign Downloads

Download and print protest signs for the Karmelo Anthony trial. Bring them to the courthouse in McKinney, TX on June 1–5, 2026, share on social media, or display in your community.

🖨️ Print Instructions — Landscape (Horizontal)
  • Set your printer to LANDSCAPE — every sign is horizontal (4:3). In the print dialog choose Orientation: Landscape and Fit to page.
  • Letter paper (8.5"×11"): print landscape = 11" wide × 8.5" tall. Clean, visible, no cutoff.
  • Tabloid (11"×17"): print landscape = 17" wide × 11" tall — maximum courthouse impact.
  • Outdoor signs: print on 110 lb cardstock, mount to foam board, attach a wooden dowel/paint stick.
  • PDF = print (vector-sharp at any size) · PNG = social (Instagram, X, Facebook) · SVG = editable.
  • Color recommended — gold & purple gradients on black for full solidarity impact.
